# ⚖️ Haki Debate AI

Multi-agent AI debate system for East African constitutional rights — two agents argue opposing interpretations of the same legal question.

Two AI agents argue opposing sides of a constitutional question — then a third synthesizes. 
In Swahili, English, or both.

## Research Basis
- **Liang et al. (2023)** — *"Encouraging Divergent Thinking in Large Language Models through Debate"* arXiv:2305.19118. Multi-agent debate produces more accurate, balanced reasoning than single-model responses. Applied here to constitutional law.
- **"Position: The Right to AI" (2025)** arXiv:2501.17899 — Argues AI access is a civil right, especially for underserved communities. This tool operationalizes that principle.
- **"AI Diffusion in Low Resource Language Countries" (2025)** arXiv:2511.02752 — Documents 20% lower AI adoption in Swahili-language countries. This tool addresses the gap.
